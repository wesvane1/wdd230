Design Principles Document

Wes Vane

White Space

Apple

Apples utilizes a ton of white, and black, space throughout their website. This amount of white space and simplicity that the site has, makes for a luxurious experience. Less is more, and for companies like Apple, Tesla, Google, and many others this empty space allows for more text, or larger images available at one given time.

Repetition

YouTube

YouTube’s website repeats itself multiple times, this allows the user to understand where they are at all times. Many people can spend hours on the site, but its familiar format helps the user to never get lost. An example, you always scroll down to find more videos, it doesn’t matter if you are on the homepage, or deep in suggested.

Alignment

MKBHD

The MKBHD site uses alignment to keep all tabs and options looking clean and organized. The whole website is designed in a way to invite the user to keep searching and not be bored. Altogether this website sharpens aesthetics, and increases engagement.
