Built in 1885, and originally used as a department store, the Folsom Hotel is rich with history and stories.

Folsom is home to many bridges and beautiful scenery, this is nothing short of breathtaking in the early morning hours.

The Folsom Lake dam was originally built in the mid 1950's, and it's primary function was flood control. It now serves a greater purpose, making a fun massive lake for families to get together and have some fun!

The Folsom city Zoo is home to many fun animals, including eagles and deer, but coolest of all, there are mountain lions and bears there too! You're sure to have fun at the Folsom City Zoo!

Folsom's historic downtown is a place rich with history. Everywhere you go there are reminders of the cities past life.
