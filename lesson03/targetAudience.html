Target Audience

The target audience of the Folsom Chamber of Commerce website, mainly boils down to two types of people. They are either new to town and want to see what this specific area has to offer, or they are dedicated members who participate regularly in town activities.

Personas

Hunter: Hunter is a future business owner and wants to see if there are any websites the city can lead him to. He hopes to find a place to register his business in the city, request a small business licence, and check up on any regulations or requirements for a new small business.

Riley: Ryan is looking to relocate after getting a new job. He really liked his old town, but wants to find out more about where he is moving to. He is an active participant in local city meetings and loves to contribute on topics that he is passionate about.

Scenarios

Scenario 1: Lloyd and his wife Margret like to see the weather every morning at breakfast. Typically they would look at the paper for the weather, but the city stopped delivering to his neighborhood a couple weeks ago. Determined, he visits the website to see the 7 day forecast.

Scenario 2: A local business owner needs to check to see if they are still currently listed on the chamber website, as they pay $259 a month to be highlighted on the front page.

Scenario 3: A local resident who is new to town wants to visit the site and see if there is anything happening at the nearest park this weekend, or the rest of the month.

Scenario 4: A friend group wants to know what their city is 'known for', and in their efforts they visit the chamber website to find out more.

Scenario 5: A sales company wants to check and see how saturated the market is in this specific area, they would like to open a local office if it is not too saturated.
